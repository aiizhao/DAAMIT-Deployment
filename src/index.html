About

Digital Art and Animation at MIT
[DAA@MIT] is home to many of MIT's aspiring and experienced digital artists! Our club works to foster the digital arts community at MIT by providing people with the resources and peers needed to engage with digital art.

If you're interested in making comics, animation, 3D modeling, or simply learning how to use that drawing tablet for something other than osu!, you're welcome here!

Schedule
Monthly Challenge:

Traditional Art and Acrylics at MIT [TAA@MIT] time... To participate in this month's challenge, go outside, touch grass, and make a traditional plein air drawing or painting. Even better if you do it every day of April!

Weekly Meetings:

Come draw with us on Saturdays at 3 PM EST, even over the summer! During the Spring 2025 semester, we are meeting in Room 4-253. During the summer, we hold meetings virtually over Discord VC. Contact daamit-exec@mit.edu for access to the club Discord server.
